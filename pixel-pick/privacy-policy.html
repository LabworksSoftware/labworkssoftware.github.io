Privacy Policy

Effective date: 30 March 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to Pixel Pick, published on Google Play as Color Picker - Eyedropper, by Labworks Software.

Information We Collect

Pixel Pick does not collect, store, or transmit personal information directly.

Screen Capture Access

Pixel Pick requires Android screen capture permission in order to detect and read pixel colours from your screen in real time. This functionality is required for the app to work as intended.

Screen content is processed locally on your device only. Labworks Software does not store, collect, or transmit your screen content.

Advertising

Pixel Pick may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising partners such as Google AdMob. These providers may collect or process limited device and usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies in order to serve and measure ads.

Labworks Software does not control data collected by third-party advertising providers. You should review Google's privacy information for more details on how advertising data may be handled.

Data Sharing

Labworks Software does not sell, trade, or otherwise share your personal information. Any data processed by third-party advertising providers is governed by their own policies.

Children's Privacy

Pixel Pick is not directed specifically at children, and Labworks Software does not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
